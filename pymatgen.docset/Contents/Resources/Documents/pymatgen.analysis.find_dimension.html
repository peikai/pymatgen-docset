pymatgen.analysis.find_dimension module¶

This module provides functions for finding the dimensions of connected subunits in a crystal structure. This method finds the dimensionality of the material even when the material is not layered along low-index planes, or does not have flat layers/molecular wires. See details at : Cheon, G.; Duerloo, K.-A. N.; Sendek, A. D.; Porter, C.; Chen, Y.; Reed, E. J. Data Mining for New Two- and One-Dimensional Weakly Bonded Solids and Lattice-Commensurate Heterostructures. Nano Lett. 2017.
